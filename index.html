Sample Recipes

Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients: 200g chicken breast, 1 bell pepper, 1 cup broccoli, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 garlic clove, 1 tsp ginger

Steps:
1. Slice chicken and vegetables thinly.
2. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute.
3. Add chicken, cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
4. Add vegetables and soy sauce, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender.

Tips: Serve with rice or noodles for a complete meal!

Veggie Pasta

Ingredients: 200g pasta, 1 zucchini, 1 cup cherry tomatoes, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 garlic clove, 1/4 cup parmesan, basil leaves

Steps:
1. Boil pasta until al dente; drain.
2. Sauté garlic and zucchini in olive oil for 3 minutes.
3. Add halved cherry tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
4. Toss with pasta, sprinkle with parmesan and basil.

Tips: Use whole wheat pasta for extra fiber!

Chocolate Banana Smoothie

Ingredients: 2 bananas, 1 cup milk, 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tbsp honey, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:
1. Peel and slice bananas.
2. Blend bananas, milk, cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla until smooth.
3. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Tips: Add a scoop of protein powder for a post-workout boost!

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients: 2 slices bread, 2 slices cheddar cheese, 1 tbsp butter, 1 tomato (optional)

Steps:
1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
2. Place cheese (and optional tomato slices) between unbuttered sides of bread.
3. Heat a skillet over medium heat; grill sandwich for 2-3 minutes per side until golden and cheese is melted.
4. Serve hot.

Tips: Add a pinch of garlic powder to the butter for extra flavor!
